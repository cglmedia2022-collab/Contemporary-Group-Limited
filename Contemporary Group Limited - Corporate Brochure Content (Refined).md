## 1. Corporate Profile Overview

Contemporary Group Limited (CGL) is a dynamic leader in the Nigerian construction value chain, offering a full range of services in the construction industry. With over 30 years of experience and more than 200 completed projects, CGL has established itself as a foremost property development company in Nigeria. The group comprises:

*   **Contemporary Design Associates**
*   **Contemporary Properties Limited**
*   **Conwave Ventures Limited**
*   **Contemporary Ventures & Resources Limited**
*   **Contemporary Properties Inc. Canada**

CGL has received several accolades, including the West African Excellence in Property Development and Engineering (2007 and 2008) and the Nigeria World Class Architectural Design Consultant of the year by the Institute of Direct Marketing of Nigeria.

## 3. Members of Group

CGL's strength lies in its diverse subsidiaries, each contributing specialized expertise:

### Contemporary Design Associates (CDA)
CDA is the consultancy arm of the group, handling architectural and other forms of consultancies for private, government, and corporate clients. It also plays supervisory roles in the management and execution of the Group’s projects. CDA was the sole consultant for the Federal Government in 2007 for monitoring and evaluation of all debt relief-funded projects in Delta State through the Millennium Development Goal initiative (MDG).

**Notable Projects by CDA:** Asaba International Airport, the new Delta State Secretariat, Federal Road Safety project Asaba, Standard Organisation of Nigeria HQ Lagos and Ekiti, Nigerian Law School Enugu, Appeal Court Enugu, Governor’s Lodge Gombe, and numerous others.

### Contemporary Properties Limited (CPL)
Incorporated in 1990 (RC 167516), CPL began as an architectural consultancy and evolved into a property development company. With over 32 years of experience, CPL has been involved in the construction and development of many top-class properties across Nigeria, becoming a major player in the building industry.

### Contemporary Ventures & Resources Limited (CVR)
CVR offers a multitude of professional options, including residential and commercial development, sales, leasing, energy solutions, alternative backup power, and joint venture partnerships/financing. CVR is a subsidiary of Contemporary Group Ltd, a Design and Build Group.

### Conwave Ventures Limited
Conwave is the investment and trade outfit of the group, with foreign business partners globally. Its activities include import and export trade and investment in the agro-allied industry.

### Contemporary Properties Inc. Canada
This is a foreign affiliate responsible for offshore investments.

## 4. Our Services & Work Processes

### Our Services (Offered by CPL)
Contemporary Properties Limited (CPL) offers a comprehensive range of services tailored to client needs:

*   **Investigation:** Reconnaissance Surveys, Project Identification, Site Location Studies, Resource Inventories, Market Surveys, Land Use/Population Studies, Settlement Pattern Infrastructures, and Environmental Studies.
*   **Planning:** Basic Data Collection, Analysis Of Limiting Factors And Constraints, Concept And Methodology Formulation, Development Options, Goals And Strategies, Sectorial Planning, Physical Planning, Integrated Development Planning, Regional And Master Planning, Project Evaluation.
*   **Construction:** Wholesome Construction Activities, Turnkey Contract Administration, Construction Management, Site Supervision, and Quality Control.

### Our Work Processes
Our systematic approach ensures efficient project delivery:

1.  Obtain the client’s brief.
2.  Assessment of the client’s brief.
3.  Formulation of preliminary design parameters.
4.  Development of Design concept.
5.  Approvals from the client.
6.  Production of working drawings.
7.  Production of work plan and schedules.
8.  Identification and engagement of professionals needed to implement the process.
9.  Management of the construction and implementation process to achieve the desired effect.
10. Feedback from the client throughout the entire work process.

**Project Delivery Time:** Determined by the type of design, scope of the project, and source of material. All projects are usually delivered before the scheduled completion time.
